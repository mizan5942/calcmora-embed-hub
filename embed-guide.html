What Is a Calculator Embed?

An embed displays a calculator from CalcMora inside a page on your own website, while CalcMora continues to provide the calculation experience.

Embedding is useful when your audience needs a quick answer without leaving your article or lesson. For example, a teacher can include a conversion calculator beside a classroom activity, while a blogger can place a finance or measurement calculator next to a relevant guide.

You simply place an iframe inside your webpage, post editor, or custom HTML block. Visitors can then use the calculator directly where they are reading your content.

Helpful Embedding Tips

Use these good practices to keep the calculator useful, readable, and appropriate for your visitors.

  • Embed a calculator only when it is relevant to the subject of the page.
  • Keep the iframe width at 100% so it works well on mobile devices.
  • Use a descriptive title attribute so visitors and screen readers understand the tool.
  • Set a height that gives the calculator enough room to display comfortably.
  • Test the final page on both desktop and mobile before publishing it.
  • Place a short explanation near the embed so visitors understand what the calculator helps them calculate.
